PROXY SERVICE

Buy from Japan &
ship to Malaysia or Singapore

Many Japanese shops don't ship internationally. HAKUSAKU can help you purchase your items from online stores and even physical shops while she's here in Japan. The items will be shipped domestically to HAKUSAKU's address, and she will then pack your items carefully before shipping the items internationally to you with tracking number provided.

WORKFLOW

How does it work?

Animate, PIXIV Booth, Toranoana, Rakuten, Amazon Japan, drug stores, tickets, etc..... Just send the details of the items you want to buy & HAKUSAKU will try her best to get it for you!

01

Search

Find your item on the net & provide HAKUSAKU the details of the product you want: URL, images, price in JPY, instructions, etc. A quotation of the product including tax & domestic shipping fee will be given to you.

02

Order + First Payment

An invoice of the total cost including tax & domestic shipping fee will be sent to you. HAKUSAKU will only buy the items AFTER receiving your payment.

Fees and Charges ＞

03

Storing & Packaging

Once HAKUSAKU order the items on your behalf, they will be shipped to her address, where you can combine them with other items & have them shipped altogether. So, you can cut some costs on the international shipping fee!

04

Final Payment + Shipping

Your parcel will be weighed & an invoice of the shipping fee will be sent to you. After receiving your final payment, your item will be shipped to you. You may choose from EMS or SAL for shipping method by Japan Post with tracking service.
